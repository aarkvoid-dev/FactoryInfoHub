1. About Fashion Chemistry

Fashion Chemistry is a fashion industry information platform. We share blogs, Establishment listings, and provide paid access to Establishment contact details. Our goal is simple — to help fashion designers, brand owners, exporters, and manufacturers connect more easily. We do not manufacture products or directly handle business transactions between users and factories.

2. What You Are Purchasing

When you make a payment on our website, you are purchasing access to contact details, Establishment listing information, and industry-related insights. We are selling information, not guaranteeing business results. All final decisions and business agreements happen directly between you and the Establishment.

3. Factory Visit Is Important

We strongly recommend visiting the Establishment before starting business. During your visit, you should check production capacity, review quality, confirm legal documents (GST, IEC, etc.), and inspect samples before bulk orders. Personal verification helps build trust and reduces risk.

4. What “Verified” Means

If a Establishment is marked as Verified, it means our team visited the Establishment location, photos and videos were captured during the visit, and basic working operations were observed at that time. Verification reflects the situation on the day of visit. Business conditions may change later, so we still encourage your own verification.

5. Non‑Verified Listings

Some factories may not yet be verified by our team. In such cases, information is shared based on available data. Users should conduct their own checks. If you visit a Establishment and confirm its authenticity, you are welcome to inform us so we can review and update the listing.

6. Photos & Videos

Photos, videos, and product images are shared for reference purposes. They help you understand the Establishment setup, but we recommend checking samples and discussing details directly before placing orders.

7. Nearby Search Recommendation

We encourage users to work with nearby factories when possible. Local visits make communication easier and reduce misunderstandings. If you choose to work remotely or online, please proceed carefully and make informed decisions.
